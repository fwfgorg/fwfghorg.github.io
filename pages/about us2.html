Executives


RODNEY - CEO

Rodney strongly believes that scietific research knowledge and policies must be made easily accessible and understandable to the general Ghanaian populace, for such knowledge to be utilized for life improvement. "Is it not for this reason that scientific research works are funded and conducted in the country?"

His expertise and interest encompasses microbiology; plant pathology; food safety; invasive species; environmental biology; and sustainable agriculture.

Rodney holds BSc. Agriculture/Soil Science and MPhil. Plant and Environmental Biology (Microbiology and Phytopathology) degrees from UCC and UG, Legon, respectively.

Certificates in his possession include food safety for nutritionists and other health workers; Food safety risk assessment; Science communication; Systems thinking for sustainable development; and water and irrigation.

He was a graduate research assistant at the Institute for Environment and Sanitation Studies, University of Ghana.



NELSON - General Secretary

Nelson is a research food scientist, with expertise and interest spanning from food and nutrition; Food safety and risk assessment; Food processing; and Sustainable food production. microbiology.

Nelson holds BSc. and MPhil. degrees in Food science from the University of Ghana, legon.



OLIVE - Financial Secretary

Olive is an astute Business and Financial Administrator by profession and qualication. Her interest and contribution in managing the finances and managerial roles of FWFG is enormous; with the aim of promulgating preventive health practices among the Ghanaian populace.

Olive possesses a BSc. Business Administration from the University of Ghana, Legon.



MABEL - Programmes Secretary

Mabel loves to equip herself with knowledge and skills in food and agriculture in order to best assist in sustainable food production in Ghana; and mke a difference in the lives of our hardworking farmers and other food producers.

Mabel is an MPhil. candidate in Soil Science (Soil microbiology) at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Kumasi. She had her BSc. Agriculture at the same University and proceeded to work as a Teaching and Research Assistant at the Department of Crops and Soil Science, KNUST.

Mabel's areas of expertise include food and agriculture; soil science; and sustainable food production.



CHRISTABELL -Deputy Programmes Secretary

Christy is an entrepreneur, food safety advocate and one of the active 'ambassador' and product of the University of Ghana Food Safety Training Centre for nutritionists and other health professionals.

Her passion in ensuring that safe food and water are ingested by a consumer cannot be overemphised.

WHO WE ARE?

  • We are a non-governmental organisation which earnestly seek to uphold preventive health values and practices among the Ghanaian populace and policy makers, by promoting the adherence to acceptable food and nutritional tenets, use of safe drinking water, good hygienic and water conservation practices; through RESEARCH, TECHNICAL ADVICE, EDUCATION, ADVOCACY, CAPACITY BUILDING and VOLUNTEERISM.
