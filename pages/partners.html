  • HAKOF OGARINCS FERTILIZER COMPANY LIMITED


    MISSION

    is to ensure high and sustainable crop productivity and food security and poverty reduction through development and dissemination of environmentally sound technologies comprising mainly of improved, high yielding, and good quality varieties and better crop management.


    MANDATE OF HAKOF ORGANICS FERTILIZER COMPANY

    HOFCL is mandated to research and produce organic fertilizer which will help increase the production of food (cereals like Maize and Rice, Legumes such as Cowpea, Soybean, Groundnut, and Bambara groundnut) and industrial crops like Cocoa, coffee, Kola, Shea nut, Oil palm, and Coconut. For the root and tuber crops, a lot of research has gone into Cocoyam, Sweet Potato, Cassava, and Yam.

    HOFCL also work on horticultural crops like Citrus, Plantain, Pineapple, Banana, Avocado, Mango, and Vegetables. The company is aimed at developing and improving soil fertility for increasing agricultural productivity to ensure food security and self-sufficiency.
